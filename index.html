Data Dynamics @ UNSW Sydney

Diving into the data big bang - do cool things that helps us manage massive data! We're here to understand user experience design, how it influences us, and machine learning. We work on a wide spectrum of problems across the areas of data mining, machine learning and pervasive intelligence, especially those that arise in the application domains of internet of things, cyber-physical systems, information filtering and recommending, and human activity recognition and modeling.
